Hello, world!

FXML is intended to create a set of tools for making Forex trading decisions based off of simple calculations of risk and machine/deep learning recommendations. A Flask app made with HTML5, Bootstrap, Python, and Flask with templating by Jinja. App is containerized with Docker for portability. Production environment uses nginx as a reverse proxy to enable SSL.

Relative Strength

First step in the process is to see the Relative Strength of currencies against each other. This tool will help you see where potential trades may exist.

News

Second step is to look for any possible upcoming news about the currency pairs you're interested in trading.

Position Sizing

Last step in the process is to size the position of your trade with the risk, cost, and target automatically calculated. Starting a trade becomes as easy as pressing a button.
